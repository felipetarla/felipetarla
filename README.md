### Synthflow AI

> Enterprise Voice AI platform for sales, support, and customer operations.

I work on the frontend layer of a Voice AI platform where teams create, test, monitor, and improve AI voice agents.

**Key work**

- Migrated the app from Bubble.io to React, reducing page load time by **80%**
- Built core interfaces for agent management, action configuration, analytics, simulations, and call logs
- Reduced average bug fix time by **83%**, from 6 days to 1 day
- Standardized **40+ reusable React/TypeScript components** across product modules

---

### Piatek Institute

> Healthcare admin platform for patient operations and clinic workflows.

Built operational tools used by clinic staff to manage patient communication, scheduling, financial workflows, and support requests.

**Key work**

- Built real-time chat with WebSocket and event-driven architecture
- Helped staff manage **900+ monthly patient requests** from one admin panel
- Reduced appointment rescheduling time from 3 minutes to 30 seconds
- Delivered a financial analytics dashboard that replaced a manual weekly workflow

---

### Oaisis AI

> Flight search platform connecting web and SMS booking experiences.

Worked on user-facing and admin interfaces for flight search, booking flows, SMS interactions, and real-time communication.

**Key work**

- Built responsive interfaces for complex flight search and booking flows
- Integrated WebSocket-based chat, reducing bandwidth usage by **10x** compared to polling
- Simplified checkout from 7 minutes to 4 minutes
- Improved admin triage with priority-based notification patterns

---

### T20Med

> Telehealth startup connecting doctors and patients through web and mobile products.

Co-founded and helped build the early product experience for a telehealth platform connecting doctors and patients.

**Key work**

- Created a web platform for doctors to manage virtual offices
- Designed the Figma prototype used as reference for the mobile app
- Contributed to platform growth to **100+ doctors**
- Participated in the Nvidia Inception Program during the startup’s early stage
